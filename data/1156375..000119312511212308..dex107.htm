Notwithstanding any other eligibility requirements specified in these grants or in the Plan, in order to be eligible to receive these grants and as a condition of receipt of payment of any earned Performance Shares under these grants, you must have entered into an agreement with the Company containing certain post-termination of employment restrictions regarding competition with the Company and non solicitation of the Company’s customers and employees prior to receiving this grant. 3. Eligibility to Receive Performance Shares. Notwithstanding any other eligibility requirements specified in these grants or in the Plan, in order for you to be eligible to receive payment of any earned Performance Shares after the end of the Performance Period, you must remain employed through the Payment and Vesting Date.

 

4. Termination of Service. If your employment is terminated by reason of death or Disability (as defined in the Plan), your eligibility for payment of the Performance Shares is governed by the terms of the Plan. If your employment is terminated for any other reason, any Performance Shares that are not paid and vested will be forfeited.
